Provident Credit Union Experiential Design

Brief

Redesign Provident's experiential environment materials to be more welcoming and inline with the updated brand standards.

My Role

Brand Manager at Provident Credit Union. I led the project to redesign Provident's community event setup.

Requirements

Update the community event tent, promo items, outfits, and create guides for management.

Strategy

Create an environment that was friendly and dynamic so that the Provident experience was aligned at every touchpoint. The exterior of the tent needed to get people's attention from a distance, there needed to be clear messaging to bring the right people in, and an interior that allowed people to engage with the promotional materials and staff.

Results

There was a lot of excitement over the new setup and when it was deployed at community events the staff reported more engagement.
