Danzante

Design

Danzante Wines updated their logo and packaging and wanted a website that reflected the friendly and modern new look. While the updated branding had the same theme and audience, the new look was radically different and allowed us to go in a completely new direction with the site.

Danzante focuses on casual entertaining and they had a significant backlog of content, we developed the new site around the entertaining blog.

For ease of use, the site is hosted on a CMS platform. With that in mind we created a general layout that all pages could follow with additional elements that could be added or replaced. This was important to allow flexibility when creating new content so as to avoid each page looking too similar.

I designed this site while at Intwine Marketing and assisted with the art direction. All design work was completed with Adobe Photoshop.
