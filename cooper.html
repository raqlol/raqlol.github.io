Cooper & Thief

Design / Development

Cooper and Thief is a new wine that breaks the mold and has a story to tell. The website needed to reflect the bold, risk taking nature of the brand and tell the story of Cooper & Thief

The concept behind the site is to take the viewer through the barrel room as they pillage barrels. A nod to the viewer that they are in on the heist.

Our task was to create an engaging site using the small amount of content originally created for the launch that allows the brand to grow.
