Callie Collection

Design / Development

Callie Collection is a new wine for "moments that stop time". The website needed to reflect that Callie is unique and perfect for easy entertaining. Our task was to convey the everyday extraordinary that can be enjoyed with Callie.

The site uses a mix of effects to convey Callie's fun brand personality. Cinemagraph video, parallax scrolling, javascript/css transitions and gifs create sections that bring the brand's personality to life.

I concept was developed with the client and my team, while at Intwine Marketing. I led the design of the project and the majority of the design and graphic work was completed with Adobe Photoshop.

Because I was the designer and developer on this project, I was able to design and then prototype work quickly to ensure that the project was on track.

The development utilized several existing libraries to achieve the variety of effects.
