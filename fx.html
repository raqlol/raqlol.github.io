Freixenet

Design / Development

Freixenet is a client I’ve worked with on a number of projects including site updates, site development, and email campaigns. The project pictured above is freixenetusawines.com, this is a new site we developed for career opportunities. The site needed to be easy to navigate and simple enough that it could support multiple brands with varied identities.

The site was developed using HTML5, CSS3 and jQuery. I designed and co-developed this site while at Intwine Marketing.

The image to the left showcases the email campaigns created for Freixenet’s quarterly newsletters. The newsletters serves to promote their campaigns and to create new media that supports the brand image. I designed, developed and created content for all email campaigns from March 2015 through October 2016 while working at Intwine Marketing.
