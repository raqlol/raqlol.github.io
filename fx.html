Freixenet

Design / Development

Freixenet is a new wine that breaks the mold and has a story to tell. The website needed to reflect the innovative, risk taking nature of the brand and tell the story of Freixenet. Our task was to create an engaging site using the small amount of content originally created for the launch that allows the brand to grow.

The concept behind the site is to take the viewer through the barrel room as they steal barrels. This creates a sense for the viewer that they're in on the heist.

I developed the concept with the client and my team, while at Intwine Marketing. I led the art direction and design of the project. All design work was completed with Adobe Photoshop.

Handling the design and development of this project allowed me to make critical design choices that I knew were achievable in development.

The original code was custom made with HTML5, CSS3 and vanilla javascript, utilizing an inverted y-axis and transitions (similar to the final site code) based on window scrolling. However, during our final user testing, we found that the horizontal scrolling was not intuitive for all users. We opted for a plugin for the homepage that achieved a similar affect to our original code and was user friendly to a broader audience.
